# Simple Color Workflow

1. Base color #c23b22
- https://dribbble.com/colors 
- http://designspiration.net/

2. Accent color #0C4E64 arba #5E6D9C
- http://paletton.com/
- Enter your base color
- Click "Add Complementary"
- Alternatively click around the icons at the top

3. Neutral colors (2-3)
- White, dark grey, light grey (optional)
- Use Dribbble and Designspiration from first step
- Use photoshop
	- Create two shapes and fill them with #424242 and #fafafa
	- Insert a color fill layer above your two shapes (Layer > New Fill Layer > Solid Color...)
	- Change that fill to your base color
	- Set the blending mode to overlay, and bring the opacity right down to between 5 and 40%
	- Use the color picker and copy your new values
